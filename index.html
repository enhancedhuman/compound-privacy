Privacy Policy

Enhanced Human ("we", "us", "our") operates the COMPOUND mobile application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data We Collect

COMPOUND collects and stores the following data that you voluntarily enter:

  • Profile information (name, height, weight unit preference)
  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Nutrition logs (food entries, calories, macronutrients, water intake)
  • Health metrics (body weight, blood pressure, heart rate, steps)
  • Bloodwork results
  • Cycle and supplement tracking data
  • Progress photos
  • Custom reminders

We do not collect data automatically from your device beyond what is required for the app to function (such as crash reports).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the COMPOUND app experience. We use your data to:

  • Display your logged information within the app
  • Calculate statistics, graphs, and personal records
  • Send you reminders you have configured
  • Process subscription purchases through Apple (via RevenueCat)

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage. If you delete the app, your data is deleted.

Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat, which communicates with Apple to verify your purchase. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier and subscription status only. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

4. Third-Party Services

COMPOUND uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and payment processing. RevenueCat receives only an anonymous identifier and purchase data from Apple. No personal health data is shared with RevenueCat.
  • Apple App Store: Processes payments for COMPOUND Pro subscriptions.

5. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device for as long as you use the app. You can delete all your data at any time from Settings > Delete All Data within the app. Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored in the app (visible in the app interface)
  • Export your data as JSON from Settings
  • Delete all your data from Settings
  • Delete your account by uninstalling the app

7. Children

COMPOUND is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page.
